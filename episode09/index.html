Episode 09: The Cult

The actual town of Sussurokawa Cove is about as beautiful as it gets. Right through the center of town is a grand cascade. It's nature aided along by some artifice, a natural waterfall enhanced with a stony path ready made to cut through the town center and go straight out to sea. A large cobblestone bridge connects the two halves of town, but it's the town hall built into the bottom of Rumeuri Hill that really seems to keep the town stuck together. If you looked at the town from the front, the grand cascade would look like a series of steps leading up to the town hall. The water flows underneath it.
